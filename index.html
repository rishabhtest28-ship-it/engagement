Strangers to Soulmates

📍 NIT Trichy · Pehli Mulaqat
Ek Anjaan Si Shuruwat

Do log. Ek college. Hazaaron students ke beech mein — NIT Trichy ke un corridors mein koi script nahi thi, koi plan nahi tha. Sirf ek anjaan chehra jo dheere dheere jaana pehchana lagne laga. Pehle ek hello, phir baatein, phir routine — aur kab dosti bann gayi, pata hi nahi chala.

🎓 NIT Trichy, Tamil Nadu
🌱 Dosti Ka Woh Daur
Yaar Bane, Dil Badla

College ki late night conversations, canteen ki chai, exams ki tension mein ek dusre ka saath — yeh woh waqt tha jab dono ek dusre ke liye khaas bante ja rahe the. Koi ek pal nahi tha jab sab badal gaya — yeh ek gehri, dheemi, honest dosti thi jo apne aap kuch aur banne ki taraf badh rahi thi.

🏙️ Pune · Jahan Pyaar Hua
Pune Mein Mohabbat

College ke baad dono ki zindagi ne Pune ka raasta pakda. Ek nayi city, nayi zindagi — aur in sab ke beech dil mein jo tha woh chhupna band ho gaya. Dosti ki us gehraai se mohabbat ka pehla sachcha ehsaas jaaga — aur dono ne jaana ki yeh dosti se kahin zyada kuch hai.

"Tu Hai Toh Dil Dhadakta Hai,
Tu Hai Toh Saans Aati Hai,
Tu Naa Toh Ghar, Ghar Nahi Lagtaa..."
🌆 Pune, Maharashtra
💌 28 November 2024
